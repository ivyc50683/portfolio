Currently a Master’s student at UCL studying Cognitive and Decision Science, my research focuses on perception, memory formation, and NLP memory modeling under Dr. Avital Hahamy's supervision. I am also involved in developing LiveSign at UCL AI Foundry Cohort 3 and volunteering with Project Impactive to design assistive technology.

Before my Master’s, I spent 6 years as a UX Researcher, Designer, and Product Owner in startups, building products from inception, including a navigation app for individuals with visual impairment.

2017: Graduation I graduated from The University of Auckland with first class honours in both a Bachelor of Arts (Hons) in Psychology and a Bachelor of Commerce in Marketing and Management.

2018: First Step into Tech I started my tech career as a Recruitment Consultant at Aspire, focusing on hiring engineers, designers, and product managers for startups and consultancies.

2018: People & Culture Specialist I joined PALO IT as a People and Culture Specialist, where I recruited engineers, designers, and product managers while fostering a vibrant company culture. This experience gave me deeper insights into role collaboration within the tech world.

2019: UX Research and Agile Consultant Leveraging my psychology background, I transitioned into a UX Research and Agile Consultant role, making meaningful contributions to client projects, particularly within startups.

2021: Product Lead at Ed-tech Startup At Big Bang Academy, an ed-tech startup backed by Alibaba Entrepreneurs Fund and Gobi Ventures, I served as their first technical hire. My responsibilities spanned company strategy, investor engagement, product direction, user research, and UI design. This role provided me with invaluable startup experience.

2022: UX Product Lead at Geotech Startup I joined Maphive Technology Limited, a geotech startup supplying indoor map data for Apple. I led the development of apps and web platforms for diverse users, including those with visual impairments, collaborating closely with engineers and designers to launch products weekly.

2024: Master's at UCL Currently, I’m pursuing a Master’s in Cognitive and Decision Science at UCL, with modules in Python programming, computational modeling of cognition, and computational linguistics. My thesis focuses on perception, memory, and NLP memory modeling.
